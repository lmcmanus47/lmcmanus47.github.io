During the spring of 2018, I worked with the New Brunswick High School Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics Club on an agent-based modeling project based the coral-algal competition model I developed during my PhD. We used the NetLogo programming language through NetLogo Web. Below are some of the materials I used during this short, informal course and project.
